About CIP

CIP Services provide an ingenious mobile food tanker cleaning solution, revolutionising the industry. Not just by taking the service to the client and therefore saving costs, but also in the quality of the service provided that has resulted in CIP becoming the industry benchmark. We develop programs for individual products and tanker types, in order to minimize resource usage and waste disposal volumes. Through adaptation of technology, this allows us to monitor the performance of the wash. The results are a greener and more efficient clean, creating a new high standard in tanker cleaning solutions. C.I.P Services are proud to be an associate to THE DAIRY TRANSPORT ASSURANCE SCHEME (DTAS) The scheme standards comprise a set of requirements to ensure food safety (including food hygiene) & traceability.
